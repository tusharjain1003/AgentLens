Ultimate Web-RAG Benchmark Questions (Top 20)

1. AI + Tech Intelligence

1. Compare OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind strategy evolution since ChatGPT launched.
   Tests: temporal reasoning, synthesis, multi-source comparison

2. Which companies benefit most if AI inference becomes dramatically cheaper than training?
   Tests: second-order reasoning, economic analysis

3. Compare NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel AI revenue growth, margins, and market positioning over the last 5 years.
   Tests: financial retrieval, numerical reasoning, trend analysis

4. Which AI startups raised the most funding recently, and what infrastructure bets are investors making?
   Tests: latest-news retrieval, aggregation


2. Finance + Economics

5. Why are analysts comparing the current AI boom to the dot-com bubble, and where does the analogy fail?
   Tests: contradiction synthesis, historical comparison

6. Compare inflation recovery trends across the US, India, and Europe after COVID-era peaks.
   Tests: numerical reasoning, macroeconomic synthesis

7. Which countries are investing most aggressively in AI infrastructure right now?
   Tests: government policy retrieval, global aggregation

8. Which streaming platform has the best revenue-per-subscriber economics today?
   Tests: business metrics comparison


3. Sports + Data

9. Which football clubs spent the most on transfers over the last 10 years, and did it translate into trophies?
   Tests: long-range aggregation, statistical reasoning

10. Compare Messi, Ronaldo, and Mbappé in terms of social media influence versus on-field performance.
    Tests: heterogeneous data fusion

11. Which IPL team had the best win-to-spending efficiency in recent seasons?
    Tests: math + retrieval + ranking


4. Media + Internet Culture

12. Which YouTubers scaled into billion-dollar businesses, and what monetization patterns do they share?
    Tests: multi-source synthesis, business analysis

13. Compare Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour economics with major global sports events.
    Tests: numerical comparison, cross-domain retrieval

14. Which celebrity controversies caused the largest measurable follower decline online?
    Tests: temporal analysis, social metrics


5. Society + Future

15. What are the strongest arguments that AGI is close, and what are the strongest counterarguments?
    Tests: contradiction handling, balanced synthesis

16. Which industries are most vulnerable to autonomous AI agents in the next 5 years?
    Tests: forecasting, reasoning, synthesis

17. What are the second-order effects of AI-generated code on junior developer hiring?
    Tests: abstract reasoning, economic impact analysis


6. Geopolitics + Current Affairs

18. How has drone warfare changed military strategy in recent global conflicts?
    Tests: latest developments, multi-source synthesis

19. Compare China, the US, and Europe in semiconductor independence efforts.
    Tests: geopolitical comparison, policy aggregation


7. Retrieval + Citation Stress Tests

20. Which sources explicitly disagree on whether remote work improves productivity, and what evidence does each side cite?
    Tests: contradiction retrieval, citation precision, evidence clustering
